ReiSearch offers multiple presentation templates. Each one serves a different purpose depending on your audience and the level of detail needed.

### Template Types

**One-Pager**
A single-page summary with the most essential information:

- Property photo and price.
- Deal type and key details.
- Brief comp summary (2-3 top comparables).
- Contact information.

Best for: Quick deal sharing, initial outreach, high-volume wholesaling. Loads fast and fits in an email preview.

**Standard**
Multi-page layout with balanced detail:

- Cover page with property photo.
- Full property details.
- Comparable sales table.
- Market value estimates.
- Demographics section.
- Deal structure.
- Notes and commentary.

Best for: Most buyer presentations. Gives enough detail for a serious buyer to evaluate without overwhelming them. Included on all paid plans.

**Full Presentation**
Expanded multi-page with maximum detail and customization:

- All Standard sections plus:
- Multiple photo gallery pages.
- Full comps table with score breakdown.
- Underwriting details (ARV, MAO, cash flow).
- Custom slides for additional context.
- Professional formatting and layout options.

Best for: Lender presentations, partner proposals, JV deals. Use when you need to present a complete investment thesis.

### Template Availability by Plan

| Template | Free | Starter/Core | Scale |
|----------|------|-------------|-------|
| One-Pager | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Standard | No | Yes | Yes |
| Full | No | No | Yes |

### Choosing a Template

- **Sending a quick deal to a buyer network** — One-Pager.
- **Presenting to a partner or investor** — Standard.
- **Pitching to a lender or private fund** — Full Presentation.

If you are not sure, start with Standard. You can upgrade to Full later without recreating the presentation from scratch.

### Custom Templates (Scale Plan)

Scale plan users can save custom templates with preferred layouts, color schemes, and default sections. Apply your saved template to new presentations for consistent branding.
