## Help Content

**Title:** Free vs. Paid Presentations
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** free-vs-paid-presentations
**Purpose:** Understand what you can generate on a free plan vs. what paid plans unlock.

**Content:**

Presentation features differ significantly between free and paid plans. Knowing the limits helps you decide which plan fits your deal volume.

### Free Plan

Free plan users can generate one-pager presentations. These are single-page summaries with:

- Property photo and price.
- Basic deal details (type, beds, baths, sq ft).
- Top 2-3 comps.
- Contact information.

**Limitations:**
- No multi-page layout.
- No demographics or market data panels.
- No custom slides.
- No full comps table.
- No underwriting details (ARV, MAO, cash flow).

Free plan presentations are useful for quick deal sharing but not for serious partner or lender presentations.

### Paid Plans (Starter, Core, Scale)

Paid plan users get full multi-page presentations with:

- Professional cover page.
- All property details and gallery.
- Full comps table.
- Market value estimates from multiple sources.
- Demographics and market data.
- Deal structure section.
- Custom slides and editing.

**Starter and Core** — Standard template access. Full data panels. No custom template saving.

**Scale** — Full Presentation template. Custom template saving. Maximum layout control and branding.

### Cost Per Presentation

- **Free plan** — Presentations consume tokens. One-pagers cost fewer tokens than full presentations.
- **Paid plans** — Multi-page presentations are included in your monthly allowance. Token limits still apply based on your plan tier.

### Wholesaler Onboarding Special

New wholesaler users receive a 25,000 token grant as part of onboarding. This grant covers your first full paid-tier presentation with all data panels included — no upgrade required for the first one.

After the grant is used, additional full presentations require a paid plan or token purchase.

### Choosing Your Plan

- Generating 1-3 presentations per month and they are mostly quick deals — Free plan may be sufficient.
- Generating 5+ presentations per month for buyers and partners — Upgrade to Starter or Core.
- Generating presentations for lenders and institutional partners — Scale plan unlocks the Full template and custom branding.
