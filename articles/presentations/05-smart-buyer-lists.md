Smart Buyer Lists let you group buyers by investment criteria so you send the right presentations to the right people.

### What Is a Smart Buyer List

A Smart Buyer List is a saved group of buyers filtered by their buy box criteria. Instead of manually selecting recipients each time, the list automatically includes buyers whose criteria match the property you are presenting.

### Creating a Buyer List

1. Go to Presentations or Network > Buyers.
2. Click "Create Buyer List."
3. Give the list a name (e.g., "Phoenix Flips" or "Buy-and-Hold Investors").
4. Set filter criteria based on buy box attributes:

- Location preference (city, ZIP, metro area).
- Price range minimum and maximum.
- Deal types they buy (Cash, SubTo, Seller Finance, etc.).
- Property types they target.

5. Save the list. ReiSearch populates it with buyers whose buy boxes match your filters.

### Managing Buyer Lists

**Viewing list members** — Open any list to see the buyers included. Each entry shows their name, buy box summary, and contact options.

**Editing list filters** — Change the criteria and the list updates automatically. Buyers who no longer match are removed. New matching buyers are added.

**Merging lists** — Combine two lists if your criteria overlap or you want a broader audience for a specific deal.

**Deleting a list** — Removing a list does not delete the buyers. It only removes the grouping.

### Sending Presentations to a List

1. Open the presentation you want to send.
2. Click "Share" and select "Send to Buyer List."
3. Choose one or more lists.
4. Review the recipients and add a personal message.
5. Send.

Each buyer on the list receives the presentation. If auto-send is enabled on their buy box, they may receive it automatically without you taking action.

### Tagging Buyers

Add tags to individual buyers to refine your lists further:

- "Hot lead" — High engagement, prioritize for premium deals.
- "Luxury buyer" — Targets properties over $500,000.
- "New connection" — Recently added, still building relationship.
- "Out of market" — Temporarily not buying.

Tags are private to your account. Buyers do not see how you have tagged them.

### List Analytics

View metrics for each list:

- Number of buyers on the list.
- Presentation open rates by list.
- Response rates from list-sent presentations.
- Most active buyers (by engagement).

Use this data to focus your deal flow on the lists that convert best.
