## Help Content

**Title:** Managing Your Marketplace Listings
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** marketplace-listings-management
**Purpose:** Edit, update, and track your published Marketplace listings.

**Content:**

Once a property is published to the Marketplace, you can manage it from the property detail page. Listings are not static. You can update them as the deal evolves.

### Finding Your Published Listings

Go to Properties > Published tab. This shows every property you have published to the Marketplace. Click any listing to open its detail page and manage it.

### Editing a Published Listing

Open the published property. Any changes you make to the property details update the Marketplace listing automatically:

- **Price** — Update the asking price. The new price appears on the property card and in search results.
- **Photos** — Add, remove, or reorder photos. The gallery updates immediately.
- **Description and notes** — Edit the property description or add notes. These appear in the listing detail.
- **Deal type** — Change the deal type (Cash, SubTo, Seller Finance, etc.). The badge on the property card updates.

There is no need to unpublish and republish. Changes apply as you save.

### Unpublishing a Listing

To remove a property from the Marketplace:

1. Open the property.
2. Click "Unpublish" or change the status back to Draft.
3. Confirm the action.

The property disappears from the Marketplace immediately. It remains in your properties list under Drafts. All data (comps, documents, notes) is preserved. You can republish later without re-entering everything.

### Responding to Inquiries

When a buyer or partner messages you about a published listing:

- Reply through the platform messaging system.
- Check the Messages icon in the header for new inquiries.
- Respond promptly. Buyers browsing multiple platforms may move on if you take too long.

You can set up automatic reply templates in Settings for common questions.

### Listing Stats

View how your listing is performing:

- **Views** — How many users have viewed the listing.
- **Inquiries** — How many messages you have received about it.
- **Saves** — How many users have saved it to their folder or watchlist.
- **Match notifications** — How many buy box matches the listing generated.

Stats are available from the property detail page or a Listing Analytics section.

### Marking a Listing as Sold

When a deal closes:

1. Open the property.
2. Change the status to Offers or mark it as Sold.
3. The listing is removed from active Marketplace search results.
4. It remains in your properties and history for record keeping.

Marking it as Sold rather than unpublishing preserves the deal history for your portfolio analysis.
