## Help Content

**Title:** Private and Unlisted Deals
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** private-listings
**Purpose:** Share properties privately without publishing them to the public Marketplace.

**Content:**

Not every deal needs to be public. You can share properties privately with specific users while keeping them hidden from the Marketplace.

### Private vs. Public

**Public listing** — Published to the Marketplace. Visible to all ReiSearch users in map and grid search. Can generate buy box matches and auto-send presentations.

**Private share** — Shared only with specific users via direct link or sharing. Not visible in Marketplace search. No buy box matches. No auto-send.

### How to Share a Property Privately

1. Open the property (from Drafts or Shared status).
2. Click "Share" or the share icon.
3. Select "Private Link" or "Direct Share."
4. Choose recipients from your Network or enter email addresses.
5. (Optional) Set a permission level: View, Edit, or Admin.
6. Send.

The property appears in the recipient's Shared tab. It does not appear in the Marketplace.

### Private Links

Generate a private shareable link:

1. Open the property.
2. Click "Share" > "Copy Private Link."
3. Send the link through email, text, or any channel outside the platform.

Anyone with the link can view the property. They do not need an active connection to you, but they do need a ReiSearch account to view it.

### Managing Private Links

- **Revoke a link** — In the sharing section, find the link and click "Revoke." The link stops working immediately.
- **Set an expiration** — Create links that expire after a set time (24 hours, 7 days, 30 days).
- **Password protection** — Some plans support adding a password to private links. Recipients enter the password to view the property.

### Access Controls

When sharing privately, you control what recipients can do:

- **View only** — See the property details, comps, and documents. Cannot edit or share further.
- **Edit** — Can update property details and add documents.
- **Admin** — Full access including the ability to share with others.

### When to Use Private Sharing

- **Wholesale deals** — Show a deal to specific buyers before the public.
- **Partner review** — Get feedback from a partner without exposing the deal broadly.
- **Confidential listings** — Properties the seller does not want widely marketed.
- **Test pricing** — Share with a few buyers to gauge interest before publishing.

### Converting a Private Share to a Public Listing

If you decide to open a privately shared property to the full Marketplace:

1. Open the property.
2. Click "Publish to Marketplace."
3. The property is now public. Previous private share recipients retain their access.
