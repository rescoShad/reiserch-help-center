## Help Content

**Title:** Collaboration Apps
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** collaboration-apps
**Purpose:** Connect collaboration tools to improve team communication and file sharing.

**Content:**

Collaboration integrations connect your team's existing tools to ReiSearch, making it easier to coordinate on deals without switching platforms.

### Available Collaboration Integrations

Available collaboration integrations depend on your plan and App Center inventory. Current and planned integrations include tools for:

- **Team messaging** — Connect team chat platforms to receive property updates and deal notifications directly in your team's communication channel.
- **File sharing** — Sync property documents with connected cloud storage for easier sharing and backup.
- **Project management** — Push property deals to connected project management tools for pipeline tracking.

### How to Connect

1. Open App Center from the sidebar.
2. Find the collaboration app you want to connect.
3. Click "Connect."
4. Authenticate with the third-party service.
5. Grant the requested permissions.

### What Collaboration Apps Unlock

Depending on the specific app, connected collaboration tools can:

- Send property updates to your team channel (new comps, status changes, shared properties).
- Sync documents between ReiSearch and your cloud storage.
- Create deal records in connected project management tools when you add a property.

### Managing Collaboration Connections

Open App Center and find the connected app. Click "Manage" to:

- View connection status.
- Adjust what data is shared.
- Disconnect the app.

Disconnecting stops data sharing but does not delete data already synced.

### Plan Requirements

Some collaboration integrations may require a paid plan. Check the integration card in App Center for specific requirements.
