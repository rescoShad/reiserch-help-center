## Help Content

**Title:** What Is the App Center?
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** New users
**Slug:** what-is-app-center
**Purpose:** Learn how the App Center extends ReiSearch with third-party integrations.

**Content:**

The App Center is ReiSearch's integrations hub. It lets you connect third-party tools to your account, unlocking features that work across the platform. Instead of switching between apps, connected tools appear where you need them inside ReiSearch.

### How to Access the App Center

Click "App Center" in the left sidebar or go to /integration. The page shows a grid of available integrations, each displayed as a card with the app icon, name, category, and connection status.

### Integration Categories

| Category | What It Does |
|----------|-------------|
| CRM | Connect your customer relationship management tool to sync contacts, leads, and deal data between systems. |
| AI | Enable AI assistants for content generation, data analysis, and platform automation. |
| Social Media | Connect social channels for sharing and promoting your properties. |
| Collaboration | Tools for team communication and file sharing within the platform. |
| Google Contacts | Import your Google contacts and invite them to ReiSearch directly from your Dashboard. |

### Current Integrations

Available integrations include:

- **GoHighLevel (REI CB CRM)** — Full CRM integration. Sync contacts and leads. Property detail pages gain a CRM button for managing contacts directly from ReiSearch.
- **ChatGPT** — AI integration for content generation and data analysis across the platform.
- **Gemini** — AI assistant integration for on-platform intelligence.
- **Google Contacts** — Import contacts and invite them to connect on ReiSearch. Accessible from the Dashboard sidebar and the Google Contacts page.

### Connecting an App

Click the "Connect" button on any integration card. Follow the authentication prompts from the third-party service. Once authorized, the card badge changes from "Not Connected" (gray outline) to "Connected" (green).

### Disconnecting an App

Open the App Center, find the connected app, and click "Disconnect" or "Manage." Confirm the disconnection. The app is removed from your account and platform features tied to it will no longer appear.

### What Changes When You Connect

Connected apps add functionality throughout the platform:

- CRM connection adds a CRM button to property detail pages and a "CRM Connected" label in navigation.
- AI connections add content generation options in presentations and data analysis tools.
- Google Contacts import populates your Dashboard sidebar with contact invite options.

### Plan Requirements

Some integrations are only available on certain plans. For example, GoHighLevel CRM requires Core ($50/mo) or Scale ($200/mo) plans. Check the integration card for plan requirements before connecting.
