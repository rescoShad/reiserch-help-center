## Help Content

**Title:** Buy Box Notification Settings
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** buy-box-notifications
**Purpose:** Control how and when you are notified about buy box matches.

**Content:**

You can control how buy box matches reach you. Choose between instant notifications, daily digests, or pause matching entirely when you are not actively buying.

### Notification Channels

Buy box matches can arrive through:

**In-app notifications** — Appear in the Notifications bell and Dashboard panel. This is always on for active buy boxes.

**Email notifications** — Sent to your account email address. Controlled by your notification preferences. You can choose instant email, daily digest, or no email.

**Auto-send presentations** — If enabled, the full presentation arrives in your presentations view and by email. Auto-send may count toward your 3-per-7-day cap.

### Setting Notification Preferences

Go to Settings > Notifications. Find the "Marketplace and Matches" or "Buy Box" section.

Options:

- **Instant notifications** — Every match triggers a notification immediately.
- **Daily digest** — All matches from the day arrive in one email or notification batch.
- **Weekly digest** — A weekly summary of all new matches.
- **Off** — No notifications for new matches. You can still check matches manually by reviewing your buy box.

### Email vs. In-App

| Preference | In-App | Email |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Instant | Yes | Optional |
| Daily digest | No | Optional |
| Weekly digest | No | Optional |
| Off | No | No |

### Frequency Controls

To avoid being overwhelmed during active deal flow:

- **Cap per day** — Set a maximum number of match notifications per day. Once reached, additional matches are held until the next day.
- **Cap per week** — Set a weekly maximum. Useful if you only review deals on weekends.
- **Quality threshold** — Only receive notifications for matches above a certain match confidence level.

### Snoozing a Buy Box

When you are not actively buying, snooze your buy box instead of deleting it.

From your buy box management screen, click "Snooze" and select a duration:

- 7 days
- 14 days
- 30 days
- Custom date

While snoozed, the buy box is inactive. No matches are generated and no notifications are sent. The buy box reactivates automatically when the snooze period ends.

### Pausing All Buy Box Matches

To pause matching across all your buy boxes at once:

- Go to Network > Buyers or your profile settings.
- Toggle "Matchmaking" or "Auto-Match" to Off.

This stops all match generation without changing your buy box criteria. Turn it back on when you are ready to receive matches again.

### Checking Matches Manually

Even with notifications off, you can see your active matches by:

- Opening your buy box management screen and viewing the "Matches" section.
- Browsing the Marketplace for properties that fit your criteria.
