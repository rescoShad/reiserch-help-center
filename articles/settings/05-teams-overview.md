## Help Content

**Title:** Teams Overview
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** teams-overview
**Purpose:** Create and manage collaborative teams for sharing properties and working on deals together.

**Content:**

Teams let you group users into a collaborative workspace. Team members can share properties, communicate in property threads, and work on deals together with controlled access.

### Creating a Team

1. Go to Settings > Teams.
2. Click "Create Team."
3. Enter a team name (e.g., "Phoenix Acquisitions" or "Deal Desk").
4. Add an optional description.
5. Click "Create."

### Adding Members

1. Open the team management screen.
2. Click "Add Member."
3. Enter the person's email address. They must have a ReiSearch account.
4. Set their permission level (see below).

Members receive a notification that they have been added to the team.

### Permission Levels

| Level | Can Do |
|-------|--------|
| View | See shared properties and documents. Cannot edit or share. |
| Edit | Update property details, add documents, run comps on shared properties. |
| Admin | Full access: edit properties, share with others, manage team membership. |

Permissions are set when adding a member and can be changed anytime.

### Team Properties

When a team member shares a property with the team:

- The property appears in every team member's Shared tab.
- Members can view, discuss, and collaborate based on their permission level.
- Property threads are visible to all team members with access.

### Team Communication

Team members can use property threads to discuss specific deals. Threads on team-shared properties are visible to all team members with access to that property.

For general team communication (not tied to a specific property), use the platform messaging system. There is no dedicated team chat channel at this time.

### Removing a Member

1. Go to Settings > Teams.
2. Find the member in the member list.
3. Click "Remove."
4. Confirm.

The member loses access to all team-shared properties immediately. Their existing connections and individual properties are not affected.

### Deleting a Team

1. Go to Settings > Teams.
2. Click "Delete Team."
3. Confirm the deletion.

All members lose access to team-shared properties. Individual properties and connections are preserved.

### Plan Requirements

Teams are available on the Team plan ($500/month) and above. Free, Core, and Scale plans do not include team features.
