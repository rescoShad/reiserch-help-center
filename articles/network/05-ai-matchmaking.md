## Help Content

**Title:** AI Matchmaking
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** ai-matchmaking
**Purpose:** Get AI-suggested connections based on your investment criteria and platform activity.

**Content:**

AI Matchmaking suggests other ReiSearch users who align with your investment goals. Instead of browsing the Directory manually, the AI identifies potential partners, buyers, and collaborators for you.

### How Matchmaking Works

The AI analyzes your profile, buy boxes, and platform activity to understand what you are looking for:

- Your buy box criteria (price range, location, deal types).
- Your expertise areas and stated goals.
- Properties you have shared, published, or saved.
- Your existing network connections and their profiles.

It then searches for users whose profiles and behavior suggest a good match:

- Buyers whose buy boxes match properties you publish.
- Sellers whose published properties match your buy box.
- Investors with complementary expertise (e.g., you wholesale and they rehab).
- Active users in your target markets.

### Where Match Suggestions Appear

Match suggestions appear in:

- **Dashboard** — A suggested connections section on the right panel.
- **Network > Matchmaking** — A dedicated page with all current suggestions.
- **Notifications** — When a high-quality match is found, you receive an alert.

### Match Quality Indicators

Each suggested connection includes a match quality indicator:

- **Strong match** — Multiple alignment points. High likelihood of a productive connection.
- **Good match** — Some alignment. Worth connecting and exploring.
- **Possible match** — Limited alignment but in the same market or expertise area.

Indicators are based on the overlap between your activity and the other user's activity, not just static profile data.

### Accepting Match Suggestions

When you see a match you are interested in:

1. Click on the suggestion to view the user's profile.
2. Review their buy box, expertise, and shared properties.
3. Click "Connect" to send a connection request. The match quality note is included with your request so they know why you are reaching out.
4. Add a personal message to improve acceptance chances.

### Declining Suggestions

If a suggested match is not relevant:

- Click "Not Interested." The AI learns from this and adjusts future suggestions.
- A user will not know you declined. They only see you have not accepted.

### Improving Your Matches

To get better suggestions:

- Complete your profile — Incomplete profiles generate lower quality matches.
- Keep your buy box current — Stale or broad buy boxes produce less relevant suggestions.
- Be active — The AI considers recent activity. A profile that has not logged in for months generates fewer suggestions.

### Privacy

Matchmaking is based on your public information and activity. Private data (drafts, passwords, billing) is not used. You can opt out of matchmaking from Settings > Privacy.
