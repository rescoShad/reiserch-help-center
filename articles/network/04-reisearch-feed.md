## Help Content

**Title:** The ReiSearch Feed
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** reisearch-feed
**Purpose:** Stay updated on network activity, property changes, and marketplace movement.

**Content:**

The ReiSearch Feed is an activity stream that shows what is happening across your network and the platform. It helps you stay informed without checking each section individually.

### What Appears in the Feed

The Feed shows a chronological list of activity items:

**Network activity:**
- New connections made.
- Connection requests received or accepted.
- Buy box updates from your connections.
- Profile changes from your network.

**Property activity:**
- Properties shared with you.
- Properties published by your network.
- Comp results and updates.
- Property status changes on deals you are involved in.

**Marketplace activity:**
- New listings in your target areas.
- Price changes on watched properties.
- Dispo Blowout events.

**System activity:**
- Token grants and awards.
- Profile milestones achieved.
- Checklist completions.

### Accessing the Feed

The Feed is accessible from the Dashboard or from the Network section. Look for the "Feed" tab or link. Some feed items also appear in the Notifications panel on the Dashboard.

### Filtering the Feed

Not all activity is equally relevant. Use filters to narrow the feed:

- **By type** — Show only property updates, network activity, or system notifications.
- **By person** — Filter to show activity from specific users only.
- **By property** — Filter to show updates on specific properties.
- **By time** — Today, this week, this month, or custom range.

### Feed Notifications

Feed items that are high priority also trigger notifications in the global header bell icon. You can control which feed items generate notifications from Settings > Notifications.

### Responding from the Feed

Many feed items are actionable:

- Click a new listing to open it in the Marketplace.
- Click a connection request to accept or respond.
- Click a shared property to open it.
- Click a comp result to view the full analysis.

This keeps you moving without navigating away from the feed.

### Feed Privacy

Your own activity appears in your network's feeds based on your privacy settings and the nature of the activity. Property drafts and private actions do not appear in the feed.
