## Help Content

**Title:** Earning Tokens Through Actions
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** All
**Slug:** earning-tokens
**Purpose:** Earn free tokens by completing platform actions and hitting engagement milestones.

**Content:**

Not every token has to be purchased. ReiSearch rewards active users with free tokens for completing key actions.

### Account Setup Tokens

Complete your Dashboard checklist to earn tokens:

- **Upload profile picture** — [PHOTO_TOKEN_REWARD] tokens.
- **Complete public profile information** — [PROFILE_INFO_TOKEN_REWARD] tokens.
- **Connect social media accounts** — [SOCIAL_TOKEN_REWARD] tokens.
- **Set your expertise** — [EXPERTISE_TOKEN_REWARD] tokens.
- **Create your first buy box** — [BUYBOX_TOKEN_REWARD] tokens.

### Referral Tokens

Invite others to join ReiSearch and earn tokens when they sign up:

- **1,000 bonus tokens** for each referral who creates a verified account.
- Track your referrals from Settings > Billing or the referral section.
- There is no limit to how many referral bonuses you can earn within the program's terms.

### Engagement Tokens

Use the platform actively to earn additional token rewards:

- **Publish your first property to Marketplace** — [FIRST_PUBLISH_REWARD] tokens.
- **Share a property with a buyer** — [SHARE_REWARD] tokens.
- **Run your first multi-agent comp** — [FIRST_MULTI_AGENT_REWARD] tokens.
- **Generate your first presentation** — [FIRST_PRESENTATION_REWARD] tokens.
- **Make your first connection** — [FIRST_CONNECTION_REWARD] tokens.

### Marketplace Activity

Active Marketplace participants earn:

- **Listing views** — Receive token bonuses when your published listings receive significant views.
- **Buyer matches** — Token rewards when your buy box generates matches.
- **Deal completions** — Token bonuses when a deal closes on the platform.

### Promotional Bonuses

Limited-time earning opportunities may be available:

- Seasonal promotions (extra tokens for specific actions during a promotional period).
- Partner promotions (token bonuses for actions related to partner integrations).
- Community challenges (earn tokens for hitting usage milestones during events).

Promotions are announced through notifications, email, and the Dashboard.

### Where to Track Token Earnings

Your Dashboard Tokens Wallet shows how you have earned tokens. Click into the wallet or go to /billing to see a detailed transaction history with the source of each token deposit.

### Token Earning Limits

Some token-earning actions have limits:

- Referral bonuses are capped per the program terms.
- One-time checklist rewards are earned once per account.
- Engagement tokens may have monthly caps.

Check the individual action for any limits displayed before you complete it.
