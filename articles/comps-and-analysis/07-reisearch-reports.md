**Content:**

A ReiSearch Report is a premium, professionally reviewed analysis that goes beyond the standard AI comps. It is designed for larger deals, partner presentations, and situations where you need a third-party verified valuation.

### What a ReiSearch Report Contains

The report includes everything in a standard comp run plus additional analysis:

- Verified comparable sales with manual review by a real estate analyst.
- Detailed underwriting with ARV, MAO, and cash flow projections.
- Professional presentation-ready format for sharing with partners, lenders, or investors.
- Market analysis with demographic context.
- Custom adjustments and commentary specific to your property.

Each report is reviewed by a real estate professional before delivery, not generated solely by AI.

### When to Order a Report

A ReiSearch Report is worth the investment when:

- You are presenting a deal to a private lender or funding partner.
- The property is unique or has limited comps that need expert interpretation.
- You need a third-party valuation for a joint venture or partnership.
- The deal size justifies the additional cost.

### Cost and Turnaround

- **Cost:** $99 per report.
- **Turnaround:** 24 to 72 hours from order submission. Standard delivery within 48 hours for most properties. Complex or unique properties may require the full 72 hours.

### How to Order

1. Open the property you want a report on.
2. Run comps first (the analyst needs the comp data as a starting point).
3. Click "Order ReiSearch Report" from the comps results page or the property actions menu.
4. Confirm the property details and provide any additional context (your target exit strategy, specific questions, or concerns about the comps).
5. Submit the order.

### What Happens After Ordering

You receive a confirmation message. A real estate analyst reviews the property, verifies the comps, and prepares the report. You are notified when the report is ready.

Completed reports appear in the property's Documents section. You can also find them in your Reports history accessible from the comps page or property detail page.
