## Help Content

**Title:** Creating Your First Presentation
**Format:** Help article
**Audience:** New users
**Slug:** first-presentation
**Purpose:** Generate a professional deal presentation from any property in seconds.

**Content:**

A ReiSearch presentation is a deal package you can share with buyers, partners, or lenders. It pulls property details, comps, and market data into a formatted document.

### Step 1: Open a Property

Start from a property that has at least:

- A confirmed address.
- A price entered.
- Comps attached (recommended for full data in the presentation).

Navigate to Properties > Drafts and click the property you want to present.

### Step 2: Open the Presentation Tool

On the property detail page, find and click "Presentations" or "Generate Presentation." This is typically in the actions area or bottom panel.

If you have not run comps yet, the system may prompt you to run comps first. Comps provide the market data that fills the presentation's analysis sections.

### Step 3: Choose a Template

Select a template for your presentation:

- **Standard** — A balanced layout with property details, comps, and market data. Available on all plans.
- **One-Pager** — A condensed single-page summary. Free plan users can generate one-pagers.
- **Full Presentation** — Multi-page with all data panels and layout options. Available on paid plans.

The template you choose depends on your audience and your plan. Start with Standard and preview the result.

### Step 4: Review and Add Content

The generated presentation includes these sections by default:

- Property overview with photo and address
- Deal structure (price, terms, ARV)
- Comparable sales table
- Market value estimates
- Demographics
- Your notes

Review each section. You can:

- Edit text fields (property description, deal notes, market commentary).
- Reorder slides.
- Add or remove sections using the editor controls.

### Step 5: Add Custom Slides (Optional)

If you need more than the default sections, add custom slides with your own content. Use the "Add Slide" button and choose a layout: text, image, comparison, or data.

### Step 6: Export as PDF

When the presentation looks good, click "Export" or "Download." Choose PDF format. The file downloads to your computer. You can also share it directly from the platform.

### Step 7: Share with Buyers

To send the presentation to buyers:

- From the Presentations page, select the presentation and click "Share."
- Choose recipients from your Network or enter email addresses.
- If auto-send is set up on your buy box, matching buyers receive the presentation automatically.

### Plan Differences

- **Free plan** — One-pager only, limited content, no full data panels.
- **Paid plans** (Starter, Core, Scale) — Multi-page presentations with comp data, demographics, market value estimates, and template options.
- **Scale plan** — Advanced templates and full tool access.
